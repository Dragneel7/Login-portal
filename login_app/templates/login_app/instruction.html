Instructions to Play WordBuilding:-

  • Word Building is a game testing players vocabulary and analysis skills
  • The game is played by 2 players where they compete against again each other and try to maximize their scores.
  • The game start with each player entering a letter in the board grid alternatively
  • Scoring:-
    • Players can enter a word horizontaly or vertically
    • The length of the longest word formed upon entering a letter by the player is counted as his or her score
    • Word are read either top-to-bottom or left-to-right
  • The word present in the dictionary are only considered as valid
  • The player scoring the maximum points win
